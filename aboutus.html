About Us

Our Story

Vietiva has been part of the Nothern Virginia community for more than five years. Vietiva was founded by Vincent Nguyen. He has dreamed of opening a restaurant that offered the cuisine of his homeland. He has opened a modest restaurant in the Eden Center, a vietnamese shopping center in Falls Church, Virginia, that offered an extensive menu of home-style Vietnamese dishes. After many successful years in Eden Center, He has moved Vietiva to a new location in Fairfax Town Center, where they enjoy the loyalty of their existing customers and continue to attract new clientele.
